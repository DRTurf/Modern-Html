Accessibility in web development:

Accessibility is the practice of making your websites usable by as many people as possible. We traditionally think of this as being about people with disabilities, but the practice of making sites accessible also benefits other groups such as those using mobile devices, or those with slow network connections.

Importance of accessibility in web development:

Building accessible sites benefits everyone: Semantic HTML, which improves accessibility, also improves SEO, making your site more findable. Caring about accessibility demonstrates good ethics and morals, which improves your public image.
